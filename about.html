/ ABOUT COA

OUR MISSION AT COA ELKHART

We nurture body, mind, and spirit by helping to overcome physical barriers, enhancing mental acuity, and befriending our clients.

WHAT WE DO

Many families struggle with juggling child and senior care. Either they don’t have time, or they don’t know how to ask for help. We offer services for seniors such as transportation, life enrichment, and in-home care, saving families time, money, and stress. When the Elkhart community needs someone they can depend on, the Council on Aging is the most trustworthy resource for seniors in town.

WHO WE ARE

Everyone deserves to age with grace and dignity, but circumstances might make it difficult for some elderly members of our community. With the help of our dedicated full-time staff and volunteers, the Council on Aging of Elkhart County is here to make that possible. Whether it be a ride to the doctor or help around the house, we are always there to lend a hand.

WORK WITH US

Are you looking for a position that allows you to make a difference in someone's life? If you enjoy serving others, are compassionate, and value the company of elder individuals, you already have the basic skills needed to help us fulfill our mission! Our mission is to keep seniors safe, healthy and in their home as long as possible. Every day with COA is a new adventure and opportunity to grow both professionally and personally!
