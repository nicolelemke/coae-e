  • Life doesn’t have to slow down. We’re dedicated to ensuring the elderly residents of Elkhart County can continue to live their best lives on their own terms while maintaining independence.

  • Everyone deserves the chance to enjoy their golden years; that’s why we’re here to help with a variety of services and a group of caring, dedicated volunteers.

  • We’re here for you! Our dedicated employees and volunteers are happy to help you or your loved one live life to the fullest with a variety of services tailored to each individual’s unique needs.

What we do to help

Life Enrichment

COA debuts exciting incentives for seniors and families in our Life Enrichment Activities Center. Our programs are designed to focus on social interaction, physical exercise, and educational activities.

LEARN MORE
In Home Care

Here for you, whenever you need it. We provide two levels of in home care services depending on your needs : Homemaking or Attendant Care.

LEARN MORE
Transportation

Let us give you a lift with our one-of-a-kind transportation services. This program exists to aid seniors who are unable to drive themselves around town. Available 6am -6pm Monday through Friday.
